FRIES RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

French fries are a globally loved snack made from potatoes that are cut into thin strips, deep-fried until golden brown, and seasoned with salt

. They are prized for their contrasting textures, featuring a crispy exterior and a soft, fluffy interior.

INGREDIENTS

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Sea salt

STEPS:

  1. Cut potatoes: Peel potatoes and slice them into evenly sized 1/4-inch thick strips.
  2. Rinse starch: Submerge the strips in cold water to wash away excess surface starch.
  3. Soak cold: Let them sit in cold water for 30 minutes to ensure maximum crispiness.
  4. Dry completely: Drain and pat the strips thoroughly dry with a clean kitchen towel
  5. Heat oil: Warm your frying oil in a deep pot to a low 150°C (300°F)
  6. Par-fry: Fry the potato strips in small batches for about 5–6 minutes until soft but not browned.
  7. Drain and cool: Remove them with a slotted spoon and let them cool completely on paper towels
  8. ncrease heat: Boost the oil temperature to a high 200°C (400°F).
  9. Flash fry: Drop the cooled fries back into the hot oil for 2–3 minutes until deeply golden
  10. Season immediately: Remove, drain quickly, and toss with salt and spices while still piping hot.
